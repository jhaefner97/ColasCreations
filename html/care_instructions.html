Cleaning and Upkeep

General Care Instructions:

  • Spot clean with a warm damp cloth.
  • Machine wash not recommended.
  • Do not tumble dry.

Is your stuffie really dirty?

  • Try adding a little soap to warm water. Spot clean with a damp cloth for smaller (but hard to get out) spots.
  • Soak your stuffie in a sink of warm water and a little soap. Gently squeeze out all of the water and let it air dry completely.

Water Balloon Care Instructions:

  • When finished playing, you can machine wash the balloons on cold, gentle cycle (garment bag recommended).
