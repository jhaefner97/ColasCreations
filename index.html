About Me

Hi, I'm Nicole!

I'm a project manager, crocheter, and founder of Cola's Creations & Gifts. But most importantly, I'm a wife and a mom to my two kids.

Most days you can find me trying to balance my life: managing projects, making sure my kids eat a vegetable every once in a while, and spending too much time picking up the toys that are strewn all over the house. This, of course, is in addition to crocheting and starting up my small business.

I find crocheting to be a great stress reliever and strive to make time for it daily. And, I must admit, the stuffies turn out so cute!

My goal is to add a little joy to everyone's lives through my crochet creations - whether that's with a custom stuffie or by giving help with one of my how-to videos. Please reach out, I'd love to hear from you!
